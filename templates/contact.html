Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our smart farming platform.

How quickly can I get started with the platform?

You can start using our platform immediately after signing up. Our onboarding process takes just 15 minutes, and you'll have access to all features during your free trial period.

Do I need technical expertise to use the platform?

Not at all! Our platform is designed to be user-friendly for farmers of all technical levels. We provide comprehensive training and support to ensure you can use all features effectively.

What types of farms can use your platform?

Our platform is designed for all types of livestock farming including dairy, beef, poultry, and pig farming. We also support mixed farming operations and can customize features for specific needs.

Is my farm data secure?

Absolutely! We use enterprise-grade security with end-to-end encryption, secure cloud storage, and comply with international data protection standards. Your data is always safe with us.

Do you offer mobile access?

Yes! Our platform is fully mobile-optimized and works on smartphones and tablets. You can access all features, record data, and monitor your farm from anywhere with an internet connection.
