About Smart Animal Farming

We're on a mission to revolutionize agriculture through technology, empowering farmers with smart solutions that increase productivity, reduce costs, and improve sustainability.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a team of passionate technologists and agricultural experts, Smart Animal Farming was born from a simple observation: traditional farming methods were not keeping pace with the demands of modern agriculture.

We started with a vision to bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and practical farming needs. Our founders, having grown up in farming communities, understood the challenges farmers face daily and saw the immense potential for technology to transform agricultural practices.

Today, we serve over 2,500 farms across Kenya and East Africa, helping farmers increase productivity by an average of 35% while reducing operational costs and improving animal welfare standards.

Mission & Vision

Our mission and vision drive everything we do, from product development to customer support.

Our Mission

To empower farmers with intelligent technology solutions that increase productivity, reduce costs, and promote sustainable agricultural practices. We believe that every farmer deserves access to modern tools that can transform their operations and improve their livelihoods.

Our Vision

To become the leading smart farming platform in Africa, enabling a future where every farm operates with maximum efficiency, sustainability, and profitability. We envision a world where technology and agriculture work in perfect harmony to feed growing populations while preserving our environment.
